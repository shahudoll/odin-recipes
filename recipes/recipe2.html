Soap Recipe 2

This is a simple and effective soap recipe that you can try at home.

whenever you want the best and perfect blend of ingredients targeting the specific problem on the skin you should definitely use the The Cold Process Method

With this method, you can create a wide variety of soaps with different scents, colors, and textures.

Feel free to customize the recipe to suit your preferences.

Follow these easy steps to get started:

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Coconut oil
  • Shea butter
  • Lye (sodium hydroxide)
  • Distilled water
  • Essential oils (optional)

Apart from the ingredients you would need some basic equipment to make the soap

these are

Equipment

  • Digital scale
  • Thermometer
  • Stick blender
  • Soap mold
  • Safety goggles and gloves

Now that you have your ingredients and equipment ready, let's move on to the instructions.

Instructions

  1. Measure the oils and butters using a digital scale.
  2. In a well-ventilated area, carefully mix the lye with distilled water, following safety precautions.
  3. Allow the lye solution to cool to around 100°F (38°C).
  4. Melt the oils and butters together until fully liquefied.
  5. Once both the lye solution and oils are at similar temperatures, slowly pour the lye into the oils while blending with a stick blender.
  6. Continue blending until you reach "trace," where the mixture thickens and leaves a trail on the surface.
  7. Add essential oils if desired, mixing thoroughly.
  8. Pour the mixture into a soap mold and let it sit for 24-48 hours to harden.
  9. Once solid, remove from the mold and cut into bars. Allow them to cure for 4-6 weeks before use.

Tips for Success

  • Always wear safety gear when working with lye.
  • Experiment with different oils and additives to create unique soap varieties.
  • Be patient during the curing process for the best results.
