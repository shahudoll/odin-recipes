Soap Recipe 3

Making your own soap can be a fun and rewarding experience. This recipe will guide you through the process of creating a beautiful and fragrant soap.

With just a few ingredients and some basic equipment, you can create unique soaps that are perfect for personal use or as gifts.

You can always modify the recipe and the ingredients to suit your preferences.

Today, we will be using the Hot Process method, which is not one of the easiest ways to make soap at home.

Hot Process of making soap-making

is a method that allows you to create soap quickly by cooking the ingredients together, resulting in a more rustic texture and appearance.

Ingredients

  • lye
  • water
  • olive oil
  • coconut oil
  • essential oils (optional)

Now that you have your ingredients ready, let's move on to the instructions.

Equipment

Procedure

  1. mix the lye with water in a well-ventilated area, following safety precautions.
  2. then add all the ingredients to the lye solution, stirring gently.
  3. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring continuously until it reaches a thick, pudding-like consistency.
  4. Once the mixture has thickened, pour it into a soap mold and let it cool for 24-48 hours.
  5. After the soap has hardened, remove it from the mold and cut it into bars.

This method is majorly used for mass production of soap in factories

Remember to always follow safety precautions when working with hot materials and essential oils. Enjoy your soap-making journey!
