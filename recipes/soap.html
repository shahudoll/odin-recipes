Soap Recipe

It is always fun and rewarding to make your own soap at home. This recipe will guide you through the process.

We always assume making soap is a complicated process, but it is quiet easy to learn how to make soaps at home

With just a few ingredients and some basic equipment, you can create beautiful and fragrant soaps that are perfect for personal use or as gifts.

You can always modify the recipe and the ingredients to suit your preferences.

there are basically three main types of soap making methods:

  • Cold Process
  • Hot Process
  • Melt and Pour

Here, today I will be teaching you the most easiest process in the making of the soaps and that is the Melt and Pour method.

Ingredients

  • Soap base (glycerin, shea butter, etc.)
  • Fragrance or essential oils
  • Colorants (optional)
  • Additives (oatmeal, dried flowers, etc.)

Now that you have your ingredients ready, let's move on to the instructions.

Instructions

  1. Cut the soap base into small cubes for easy melting.
  2. Melt the soap base in a microwave or double boiler until fully liquefied.
  3. Add your chosen fragrance or essential oils to the melted soap and mix well.
  4. If desired, add colorants and mix until evenly distributed.
  5. Incorporate any additives like oatmeal or dried flowers, stirring gently.
  6. Pour the mixture into soap molds and let it cool until solidified.
  7. Once hardened, remove the soaps from the molds and enjoy your homemade creations!

Remember to always follow safety precautions when working with hot materials and essential oils. Enjoy your soap-making journey!
